Our Services

We offer a wide range of printing services and products to choose from.

  • Offset Printing

    We have fully equiped printing machines namely Agra Platen, Adast Dominant 414, Adast dominant 515, Adast Dominant 715, Adast Dominant 755 and two mini offset machines to fulfill your needs ranging from various sizes and priorities.

  • Pre-Press Section

    We give utmost preference to the place where an idea is born and that is our pre-press section. Composing, Text setting, Image Setting and Graphics Design works are accomplished here. Well equiped computers with latest software cater to your design needs.

  • Post-Press Section

    This section gives the final touch to your product giving it an ultimate form. We have ITOH GUILLOTINE 115 Programmed cutting machine with latest features in the market. This section also comprises of stiching machine, drilling machine and numbering machine.
